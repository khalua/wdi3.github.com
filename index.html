General Assembly's Web Development Immersive class is a 12-week bootcamp that takes total programming noobs and remakes them into coding ninjas. After weeks of training, thousand of lines of code, and gallons of coffee, we're ready for the coding challenges of tomorrow.

The Class

HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, HAML, SASS, CoffeeScript, Ruby, Rails, and Ember.js are just some of the languages we know.

From day one we built as we learned and have collectively created dozens of unique projects both large and small.

From our past careers and the projects we accomplished together, we're team players and well versed in Git and Pivotal Tracker.

We learned core computer science fundamentals like data structures and algorthm processes to build a strong foundation.
